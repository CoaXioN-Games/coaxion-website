What does Lambda Fortress include?

Lambda Fortress is about playing through Half-Life 2 and other Source campaigns as the TF2 Mercs. When we say "Other source campaigns" we don't mean like Left 4 Dead and Portal, we mean other Half-Life 2 mods and map-packs. We'll try and add more compatibilities for mods over time, but most stuff that requires custom code will not be playable immediately.

Versus has always been in discussions when talking about LF, it's not something we want to have on release immediately (we'd rather get the main game in your hands first) but we'll definitely be working on it afterwards.

We have support for your TF2 inventories, letting you use all your items in Lambda Fortress. This DOESN'T mean you can use whatever weapon, item and taunt you want. You can only use what you own in TF2 already.
This does mean that if you don't own a certain weapon, you can't use it. This isn't something we're going to try and circumvent, we don't want any issues from Valve. Thankfully it's not like any weapons are really hard to get, but this may affect Free-to-Plays the most with their limited inventory space.
