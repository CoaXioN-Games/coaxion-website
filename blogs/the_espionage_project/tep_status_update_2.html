Welcome to the second development update for The Espionage Project. We know you've been waiting a while for this one, but it's been for a good reason.

The State of TEP

"As one door closes, another opens..."

In our last blog post, we made it a big point that if we were unable to recruit programmers to the team we would pivot away from developing The Espionage Project as a game. This was unfortunately the outcome that came to be. While we were dissapointed at this outcome, we are very excited to move onto this new path of development.

To better utilize the talent we already have within the team, we've decided to turn The Espionage Project into an animated short, which will be produced in Blender by our animators. In the process of this change of direction, leadership has been handed off from Not Dave or Daniel to Menoly & Aoki. The script for the short has been completed and is heavily focused on delivering something comedic for you guys. We still plan to release our character models early, but with us making these models for a Blender Animation now, we might hold off on releasing Source Versions until we find the time to port and give them all the bells and whistles they'd need.

Casting Calls

While this blog post may not have been the one you guys have been expecting, we've been exicted to share the news with you regardless, hopefully you enjoyed it.
