About Short

The Espionage Project is a short Blender fan-animation based in the universe of Valve's Team Fortress.

The short follows a duo of Secret Agents and their mission to infiltrate the building of a rival company and gain intel on the secret project being worked on within.

The short is focused on comedy and, when complete, should have a run time of about 10 minutes.
