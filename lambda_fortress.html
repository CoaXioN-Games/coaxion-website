About Game

Lambda Fortress is the iconic mod that brings the Team Fortress 2 mercenaries into Half-Life 2 and other Source campaigns for singleplayer & co-op play.

Features

-Support for most HL2 mods.
-Singleplayer and Co-Op play.
-Support for inventory cosmetics.
-A stable bug/crash free experience.
