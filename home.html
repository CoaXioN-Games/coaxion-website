Who are we?

Formed from ashes of a previous modding group, we're a collective of amateur hobbyists developing games and mods for games in our spare time.

Between our various teams, we are currently developing:

  • Deathmatch Classic: Refragged - A remake/continuation of Deathmatch Classic in the Source Engine, adding new maps, weapons, game modes, QOL improvements and more!
  • Lambda Fortress - Play Half-Life 2 and other Source campaigns as the Team Fortress 2 mercenaries, both singleplayer and co-op!
  • The Espionage Project - A stealthy TF-themed multiplayer mod. Cause coorporate espionage, or stop it!
  • Quadgoyle - The multi-platform Source Engine multi-tool!
  • TBA VN Project - A Visual Novel by Rahim Ali and MTD. More info to be announced in the future.

Partners:

  • Adastral - A unified installing solution for sourcemods, built for reliability, efficiency, speed and ease of use.
  • Fortress Connected - A bombastic conglomeration of a bunch of Team Fortress based modes, mods, and whatever the hell else we want to add to it, built off of Lambda Fortress.
