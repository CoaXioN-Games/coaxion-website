About Game

Deathmatch Classic: Refragged is a remake/continuation of Deathmatch Classic built in the Source Engine with new maps, game modes, weapons, quality of life improvements and more!

Features

-Remakes of old maps.
-All new maps.
-New gamemodes (CTF, Clan Arena, Infection & more.)
-New weapons (Railgun, BFG, Chaingun & more.)
-QOL additions (Speedometer, customizable weapon feedback & more.)
-An original soundtrack.
